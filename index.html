Drone Van Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2022, Drone Van Pvt. Ltd. is a pioneering startup in the drone industry, specializing in drone pilot training and offering a range of services. As an emerging leader in the field, Drone Van is committed to providing exceptional quality and innovation in all aspects of its operations.

Drone Pilot Training:

At Drone Van, we offer comprehensive and industry-leading drone pilot training programs. Our experienced instructors equip aspiring drone pilots with the necessary knowledge and skills to operate drones safely and efficiently. We prioritize practical hands-on training combined with theoretical learning to ensure our trainees are well-prepared for real-world challenges.

Diverse Drone Domains:

Drone Van Pvt. Ltd. is dedicated to expanding its operations and expertise across various domains within the drone industry. Currently, we have several exciting projects in the pipeline.
